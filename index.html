Our Legacy - The Story of ANSHERA

ANSHERA is not just a brand, it is a legacy built on 40 years of watch craftsmanship.

Mr. Sanaullah Ansari, the father of Mr. Abul Ansari, is a renowned name in the industry known as PRINCE WATCH SERVICE. With over 40 years of experience in watch repairing, he can bring any watch back to life with his expert hands.

Taking forward this legacy, Mr. Abul Ansari stepped in with a vision to modernize and professionalize the business. With 5+ years of experience, he founded ANSHERA. The name ANSHERA is derived from his mother Gulshan Ara Ansari - we took the alphabets from her name to create something timeless.

ANSHERA means - "It is not the watch that defines a man, but the man who defines the watch. It tells your status."

ANSHERA is built for legacy. We never put ourselves on discount. We believe only those who understand their own value will wear ANSHERA.
